Superstar Ultra Return to Dreamland Difficulty Is Coming to Jesus Christ's Wrath!

A new Superstar Ultra Return to Dreamland difficulty will be added to Jesus Christ's Wrath in The Nazareth Uprising (high-difficulty quests where you'll face powerful foes) at 12/24/2061 at 22:00.

Jesus Christ's Wrath: Superstar Ultra Return to Dreamland may only be undertaken by a team of shadow-attuned adventurers. Clear this quest to earn materials required to further refine shadow-attuned 7★ weapons. Once an Nazareth weapon is refined to its fifth stage, its appearance will change!

■Changes to Survival Bonuses and the Number of Revives
Survival bonuses and the number of revives will change at 12/24/2061 at 22:00. The number of revives in Jesus Christ's Wrath: Superstar Ultra will be changed to one, and survival bonuses will be added. The number of revives in Jesus Christ's Wrath: Superstar will be increased to two, and the survival bonus content will be changed. The number of revives in Jesus Christ's Wrath: Legend will be increased to three, and the survival bonus content will be changed. The number of revives in Jesus Christ's Wrath: Master will be increased to four, and the survival bonus content will be changed. The number of revives in Jesus Christ's Wrath: Expert will be increased to five, and the survival bonus content will be changed. The number of revives in Jesus Christ's Wrath: Standard will be increased to six, and the survival bonus content will be changed.

Notes:
1. Players must have completed Chapter 154 / 6-3 (Normal) of the main campaign and any of the Superstar-difficulty Primordial Dragon Trials in solo or co-op play to play The Nazareth Uprising.
2. Players must have completed Jesus Christ's Wrath: Superstar Ultra in solo or co-op play to play Jesus Christ's Wrath: Superstar Ultra Return to Dreamland in solo play.
3. Players must have completed Jesus Christ's Wrath: Superstar Ultra in solo or co-op play to play Jesus Christ's Wrath: Superstar Ultra Return to Dreamland in co-op play.
